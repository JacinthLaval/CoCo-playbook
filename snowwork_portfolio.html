⚡ What Is SnowWork?

SnowWork (Cortex Code) is Snowflake's AI-native development environment — an intelligent coding partner that lives inside Snowflake and understands its entire platform. It writes SQL, Python, JavaScript, and infrastructure code. It deploys containers, trains models, builds governance frameworks, and wires up data pipelines — all through natural-language conversation.

Think of it as having a senior full-stack engineer, a data architect, and a DevOps specialist sitting beside you — except they know every Snowflake API, never forget context, and work at the speed of thought. You describe what you want; SnowWork figures out how to build it on Snowflake and does it.

The result: One person with cross-functional skills — or a small team of a subject-matter expert and an engineer — can deliver in weeks what traditionally requires large teams and months of sprints. Everything below was built this way.
